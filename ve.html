Velan Coffee, often associated with Velan Kaapi, embodies the rich and traditional coffee culture of South India. Here are some key aspects of this coffee culture:

Traditional Brewing: South Indian coffee is typically brewed using a traditional filter method. The coffee is made by dripping water through a metal filter to extract a rich and strong brew, which is then mixed with hot milk and sugar to taste.

Blend of Coffee: The coffee used is usually a blend of Arabica and Robusta beans, known for their robust flavor and aromatic qualities. The beans are often roasted to a dark, rich roast to enhance the flavor profile.
